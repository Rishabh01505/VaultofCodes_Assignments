Chocolate Cake

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9-inch cake pan.
  3. In a large bowl, mix sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add milk, eggs, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients.
  5. Beat the mixture until smooth and well combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Prep time: 45 minutes | Serving size: 8 people
